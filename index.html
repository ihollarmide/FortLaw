About

We are a fully serviced legal practice that has earned a reputation for consistently exceeding the expectations of our clients. The philosophy that inspires our business strategy is to provide efficient and value driven legal services engineered to suit the individual needs of each client within the permissible limits of the law and anchored on the basis of honesty, diligence and integrity

The firm has seen the need to not only satisfy and meet the legal needs of its paying Clientele but also the legal needs of its pro bono clientele, with as much dedication in time, research and legal service.

By consistently evolving means to satisfy the needs of our clients, we take full advantage of the latest technological business support tools to deliver on-demand services to our clients in record time.

Practice Areas

Family Law

We provide legal service on issues relating to family relationship, such as adoptions,divorce and pre-nuptial agreements amidst others

Our services cover:

  • - Divorce (contested and uncontested)
  • - Custody and child support
  • - Adult guardianship & trusteeship
  • - Minor guardianship
  • - Spousal support
  • - Emergency protection orders
  • - Pre-nuptial and separation agreements
  • - Property division
  • - Child welfare

Real Estate

Whether you're planning to buy or sell your property, it is one of our areas of practice to help you with your real estate transaction. At Fort Law Office, we value your business. We offer a free consult to help you review your Contract and all other documents before you commit yourself . We specialize in the sale and purchase of property, mortgage refinancing, construction and much more. We are accessible, have competitive rates and an extremely fast turnaround.

Team

Tolulope Hans Aladejebi
SWORN ADVOCATE

Tolulope Hans Aladejebi has over ten years post-call experience from two jurisdictions (Alberta-Canada and Nigeria) having obtained his Bachelor of Law's degree from Igbinedion University, Nigeria in 2006. He was called to the Nigerian Bar in 2007. Hans practised law in Nigeria for 5 years prior to relocating to Canada with his family. In 2013, Hans moved to Canada. He did his articling with City Law Offices and was admitted to the Alberta Bar in 2016.

Hans represents clients in a wide range of contentious and non-contentious matters including but not limited to: family law, divorce action, child and spousal support. In addition to the above, Hans has successfully represented clients before the Canadian Immigration and Refugee Board advocating in citizenship, immigration and refugee matters.

A father of two, Hans is a member of the Knight of Columbus and also a member of the Fort Saskatchewan Multicultural Society.
